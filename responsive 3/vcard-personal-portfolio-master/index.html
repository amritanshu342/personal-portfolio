About Us

Gyandeep High School is Affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi was established in the year of 2009 aiming at imparting moral based value education to the future generation. The school follows English-medium, Co-education system.

The school is run by Patlawati development Trust,Patna(Regd.). During a short span of time the school became popular in Digha for its quality education. It works on the lines of Shanti-Niketan. The Gyandeep students have established their supremacy in many activities and have won many prizes.
